My House Plants

Titanopsis calcarea is a mat-forming succulent with basal rosettes up to 3 inches (7.5 cm) in diameter. The fleshy leaves are up to 1 inch (2.5 cm) long, with truncate tip, usually grey or blue-green and densely covered at the apex with whitish, creamy-grey, ocher, red or bluish, rough tubercles.

  • Flowers - yellow to orange.
  • Size - 3in dia.
  • Sun - Full sun.
  • Soil - Porous, gritty and well-drained potting soil.
  • Humidity - Concrete Leaf prefers a dry atmosphere.
  • Nutrients - Feed with a cactus food every 1-2 weeks during the growing season in the spring.
  • Hardiness - 25F
